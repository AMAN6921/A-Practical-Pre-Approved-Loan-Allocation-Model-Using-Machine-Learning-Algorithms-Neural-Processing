About the System

The Problem

190 million Indians remain unbanked, with 65% of the rural population lacking formal credit access. Traditional moneylenders charge 24-36% interest rates, while banks offer 10-12% but reject most rural applicants due to lack of documentation and credit history.

About PALP AI

PALP AI (Pre-Approved Loan Prediction) is an AI-powered system that analyzes 11 financial indicators using 5 machine learning models to provide fair, unbiased loan assessments in under 2 seconds. We match applicants with suitable government schemes and provide personalized credit improvement plans.

Technology Stack

Backend: Python, Flask, SQLite
ML Models: XGBoost, Random Forest, Neural Networks
Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript
API: RESTful with JWT Authentication
